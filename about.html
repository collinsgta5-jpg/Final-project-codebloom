About Me

I'm a Design Technologist

I’m a passionate Design Technologist, student, and dedicated tech enthusiast with a deep obsession for the intersection of design and technology. I thrive on crafting web experiences that are not only visually compelling but also intuitive, performant, and user-centered. My work focuses on blending creative design sensibilities with robust technical execution to build digital products that look exceptional while functioning seamlessly across devices and platforms.

As a lifelong learner, I’m constantly pushing myself to master new tools, frameworks, and concepts in frontend development, UI/UX design, motion graphics, and emerging web technologies. Every day brings fresh opportunities to experiment, prototype, and solve complex problems. This continuous journey of discovery is what fuels my enthusiasm — there’s always something new to build, refine, or figure out, turning challenges into exciting creative adventures.

I’m committed to bridging the gap between beautiful design and flawless functionality, creating inclusive and impactful digital experiences that delight users. Whether through personal projects or collaborative work, I remain driven to grow alongside the evolving web landscape and contribute meaningfully to innovative tech solutions.
